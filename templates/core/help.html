Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password:

  1. Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and follow instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact your system administrator.

You can update your profile information by following these steps:

  1. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
  2. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click the "Edit Profile" button
  4. Update your information in the form provided
  5. Click "Save Changes" to apply your updates

Note: Some information may require HR approval to change. Contact your HR representative for assistance with these fields.

The dashboard provides an overview of key information and quick access to various features:

  • Top Navigation Bar: Access your profile, notifications, and logout options
  • Side Navigation: Navigate between different modules (Employees, Attendance, Payroll, etc.)
  • Dashboard Widgets: View key metrics and statistics at a glance
  • Recent Notifications: See your latest notifications
  • Quick Actions: Perform common tasks without navigating to specific modules

You can customize your dashboard view by clicking the "Customize" button in the top-right corner of each widget.

The HR Management System works best with:

  • Web Browsers: Chrome (recommended), Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
  • Internet Connection: Stable broadband connection
  • Screen Resolution: Minimum 1280x720 pixels
  • Mobile Devices: iOS 13+ or Android 9+

For optimal performance, we recommend using Google Chrome and keeping your browser updated to the latest version.

To add a new employee to the system:

  1. Navigate to the Employees module
  2. Click the "Add Employee" button
  3. Fill in the required information across all tabs (Personal, Contact, Employment, etc.)
  4. Upload any required documents
  5. Click "Save" to create the employee record

The system will automatically generate an employee ID and create login credentials that will be sent to the employee's email address.

To upload documents for an employee:

  1. Go to the employee's profile page
  2. Click on the "Documents" tab
  3. Click "Upload Document" button
  4. Select the document type from the dropdown
  5. Choose the file from your computer
  6. Add any relevant details like expiry date or description
  7. Click "Upload" to save the document

Supported file formats include PDF, DOC/DOCX, JPG, PNG, and TIF. Maximum file size is 10MB per document.

To transfer an employee to another department:

  1. Navigate to the employee's profile
  2. Click on the "Employment" tab
  3. Click "Initiate Transfer" button
  4. Select the new department from the dropdown
  5. Enter the effective date of transfer
  6. Provide a reason for the transfer
  7. Click "Submit Transfer Request"

The transfer request will be sent to the appropriate managers for approval. Once approved, the employee's department will be updated automatically on the effective date.

To process an employee termination:

  1. Navigate to the employee's profile
  2. Click on the "Employment" tab
  3. Click "Initiate Termination" button
  4. Enter the last working date
  5. Select the reason for termination from the dropdown
  6. Complete the exit checklist
  7. Upload any required termination documents
  8. Click "Submit Termination Request"

Note: Terminating an employee will trigger several automated processes including final settlement calculation, access revocation, and exit interviews. Ensure all required approvals are obtained before initiating termination.

There are multiple ways to mark your attendance:

  1. Web Check-in: Click the "Check In" button on your dashboard
  2. Mobile App: Use the mobile app's check-in feature with location verification
  3. QR Code: Scan the office QR code using the mobile app
  4. Biometric: Use the biometric scanner if available at your location

Remember to also check out at the end of your workday using the same method you used to check in.

To apply for leave:

  1. Navigate to the Attendance module
  2. Click on "Apply for Leave" button
  3. Select the leave type (Casual, Sick, Earned, etc.)
  4. Enter the start and end dates
  5. Specify if it's a full day or half day leave
  6. Provide a reason for the leave
  7. Upload any supporting documents if required
  8. Click "Submit" to send the leave request

Your leave request will be sent to your reporting manager for approval. You can check the status of your leave requests in the "My Leaves" section.

As a manager, you can approve leave requests by:

  1. Navigate to the Attendance module
  2. Click on "Leave Approvals" tab
  3. View the list of pending leave requests from your team members
  4. Click on a request to view details
  5. Check the employee's leave balance and request details
  6. Click "Approve" or "Reject" based on your decision
  7. Add comments if necessary, especially for rejections
  8. Click "Submit" to confirm your decision

The employee will be notified of your decision automatically. You can also set up delegation for leave approvals when you're on leave yourself.

If you need to correct an attendance record:

  1. Navigate to the Attendance module
  2. Click on "My Attendance" tab
  3. Locate the incorrect attendance record
  4. Click on the "Request Correction" button
  5. Enter the correct check-in/check-out times
  6. Provide a reason for the correction
  7. Submit the correction request

Your request will be sent to your manager for approval. Once approved, your attendance record will be updated accordingly. Note that attendance correction requests older than 30 days may require additional approval from HR.

To view and download your salary slip:

  1. Navigate to the Payroll module
  2. Click on "My Salary" tab
  3. Select the month and year from the dropdown
  4. Click "View Salary Slip" button
  5. You can download the salary slip as PDF by clicking the "Download" button

Salary slips are typically available within 3 days after the salary is credited to your account. You can access salary slips for the past 24 months.

To update your bank account information:

  1. Navigate to your Profile page
  2. Click on the "Financial" tab
  3. Click "Update Bank Details" button
  4. Enter your new bank account information
  5. Upload a canceled cheque or bank statement for verification
  6. Submit the request

Your request will be reviewed by the HR/Finance team. Once approved, your bank details will be updated for the next salary cycle. Please ensure you submit changes at least 10 days before the salary processing date.

To view your tax deductions:

  1. Navigate to the Payroll module
  2. Click on "Tax Information" tab
  3. Select the financial year from the dropdown
  4. View the detailed breakdown of your tax deductions
  5. You can download your Form 16 and other tax documents from this page

The system calculates tax based on your salary structure and the tax declarations you've submitted. If you notice any discrepancies, please contact the Finance team.

To submit your tax declaration:

  1. Navigate to the Payroll module
  2. Click on "Tax Declaration" tab
  3. Select the financial year
  4. Fill in details for each applicable section (HRA, LTA, investments, etc.)
  5. Upload supporting documents for each declaration
  6. Review all entries before submission
  7. Click "Submit Declaration" button

Ensure you submit your declaration before the deadline communicated by the Finance team. You can edit your declaration until it's locked for processing.

To apply for company accommodation:

  1. Navigate to the Accommodation module
  2. Click on "Apply for Accommodation" button
  3. Fill in the application form with your requirements
  4. Select your preferred location and accommodation type
  5. Specify your move-in date preference
  6. Add any special requirements or notes
  7. Submit your application

Your application will be reviewed by the Accommodation team. Allocation is based on availability, eligibility, and priority. You'll be notified once your application is processed.

To submit a maintenance request for your accommodation:

  1. Navigate to the Accommodation module
  2. Click on "My Accommodation" tab
  3. Click "Submit Maintenance Request" button
  4. Select the category of the issue (Plumbing, Electrical, etc.)
  5. Describe the problem in detail
  6. Upload photos of the issue if applicable
  7. Indicate the severity of the issue
  8. Submit your request

You'll receive updates on the status of your maintenance request through notifications. For emergency issues like water leaks or electrical failures, please also contact the accommodation manager directly.

To check out from your company accommodation:

  1. Navigate to the Accommodation module
  2. Click on "My Accommodation" tab
  3. Click "Request Check-out" button
  4. Enter your planned check-out date
  5. Provide a reason for check-out
  6. Submit your request

The accommodation team will schedule an inspection of the unit before your check-out. Any damages beyond normal wear and tear may be charged. Ensure all personal belongings are removed and the unit is clean before the final inspection.

Accommodation charges are calculated based on several factors:

  • Accommodation Type: Different types have different base rates
  • Location: Rates vary by location
  • Employee Grade: Subsidy may apply based on your employment grade
  • Occupancy: Single vs. shared accommodation
  • Utilities: Some utilities may be charged separately or included

The charges are typically deducted directly from your monthly salary. You can view a detailed breakdown of your accommodation charges in the "My Accommodation" section.

The location tracking system works as follows:

  • The mobile app uses GPS to track your location during work hours
  • Location is only tracked when you're checked in for work
  • Your location is updated at regular intervals (typically every 15-30 minutes)
  • The system creates a geofence around your assigned work location
  • Alerts are generated if you move outside the geofence during work hours

Your privacy is important - location data is only visible to authorized managers and is automatically purged after 30 days.

To manage location tracking settings:

  1. Open the mobile app
  2. Go to "Settings" > "Location Tracking"
  3. Toggle the "Enable Location Tracking" switch

Note: If your role requires location tracking, disabling it may affect your attendance recording and may require manager approval. Some roles cannot disable location tracking due to operational requirements.

As a manager, to view your team's location:

  1. Navigate to the Tracking module
  2. Click on "Team Tracking" tab
  3. View the map showing your team members' current locations
  4. Use filters to view specific team members or time periods
  5. Click on a team member's icon to view their details and status

You can also generate location history reports for your team members for specific date ranges.

If you're experiencing location accuracy issues:

  1. Ensure your device's GPS is enabled and functioning properly
  2. Check if you have granted the app permission to access your location
  3. Try restarting the app or your device
  4. Ensure you're not in an area with poor GPS signal (like inside buildings)
  5. If issues persist, report the problem through the app's "Report Issue" feature

The system has built-in tolerance for minor GPS inaccuracies. If you're experiencing consistent issues, contact your manager or the IT support team.

To generate reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module
  2. Select the report category (Attendance, Payroll, Employee, etc.)
  3. Choose the specific report type
  4. Set the parameters (date range, departments, employees, etc.)
  5. Click "Generate Report" button
  6. View the report online or download in your preferred format

Reports can be exported in various formats including PDF, Excel, CSV, and HTML. You can also schedule recurring reports to be automatically generated and emailed to you.

To schedule recurring reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module
  2. Configure your report as usual
  3. Instead of "Generate Report", click "Schedule Report" button
  4. Set the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
  5. Choose the delivery method (email, system notification)
  6. Specify recipients if sending via email
  7. Set the start date and end date (if applicable)
  8. Click "Save Schedule" to confirm

You can view and manage all your scheduled reports in the "Scheduled Reports" tab. You can edit or delete schedules at any time.

To create custom reports:

  1. Navigate to the Reports module
  2. Click on "Custom Reports" tab
  3. Click "Create New Report" button
  4. Select the data source (module)
  5. Choose the fields you want to include
  6. Set up filters to narrow down the data
  7. Configure sorting and grouping options
  8. Preview the report to ensure it meets your requirements
  9. Save the report with a descriptive name

Custom reports can be saved for future use and shared with other users who have appropriate permissions. You can also use the advanced query builder for more complex reports.

To interpret dashboard analytics:

  • KPI Cards: Show key metrics with color-coded indicators (green = good, red = needs attention)
  • Trend Charts: Display data over time to identify patterns and anomalies
  • Distribution Charts: Show breakdowns by department, location, etc.
  • Comparison Views: Compare current period with previous periods

Hover over any chart element to see detailed information. You can also click on most charts to drill down into more detailed reports. Use the date range selector to adjust the time period for analysis.

Troubleshooting

I can't log in to my account

If you're having trouble logging in, try these steps:

  1. Ensure you're using the correct email address and password
  2. Check if Caps Lock is turned on
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try using a different browser
  5. Use the "Forgot Password" link to reset your password

If you still can't log in, contact the IT support team.

I'm locked out of my account

Your account may be locked after multiple failed login attempts. To resolve this:

  1. Wait for 30 minutes - the system automatically unlocks accounts after this period
  2. Contact your manager or the HR team to request an account unlock
  3. Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password, which will also unlock your account

The app keeps crashing

If the mobile app crashes frequently, try these solutions:

  1. Ensure you have the latest version of the app installed
  2. Restart your device
  3. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > HR Management > Storage > Clear Cache)
  4. Uninstall and reinstall the app
  5. Check if your device meets the minimum requirements
Location tracking is not working

If location tracking isn't working properly:

  1. Ensure location services are enabled on your device
  2. Grant the app permission to access your location
  3. Check if you have a stable internet connection
  4. Ensure battery optimization is disabled for the app
  5. Restart the app and try again

My check-in/check-out is not being recorded

If your attendance is not being recorded correctly:

  1. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  2. Check if the system shows any error messages
  3. Verify that your location services are enabled (if using GPS-based check-in)
  4. Try using a different check-in method if available
  5. Submit an attendance correction request if the issue persists
My leave balance is incorrect

If your leave balance appears incorrect:

  1. Check your leave history to ensure all leaves are correctly recorded
  2. Verify your employment start date, as leave accrual is based on this
  3. Check if any policy changes have affected leave calculations
  4. Contact HR if you believe there's an error in your leave balance
